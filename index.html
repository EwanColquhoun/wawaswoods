About Us

Established in 2019, Wawas Woods is a family run business that prides itself on high standards of build, care and customer experience. Passion for creation runs in the family, and using tools passed down through the generations as well as the most up to date methods, we are here to help you create your own piece of family history.

Set up in the grounds of the family home, the workshop is small but bursting with ideas and inspiration to help turn your project into something that can stand the test of time.

Get in touch to see how we can help create your ideas take-off and become reality. All projects are bespoke designs and custom made to order.
